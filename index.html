Malik Edition - Health Coverage Script (Full)

Intro

Hey [Client Name], it’s Malik with US Health—just following up on your request for health coverage. I’ll keep this super quick—do you have a minute right now?

Discovery

What had you looking into coverage? Did something change with your job or your last plan?

When’s the last time you had coverage, and what type was it—employer, Obamacare, or something else?

What did you like or dislike about it? And what kind of budget are you working with?

Objections

If covered: "Perfect, just to help slow down the calls, was that through work or the Marketplace?"

If Obamacare: "Are you getting any financial assistance through that? Any pre-existing conditions?"

If Employer: "How much are you paying? And is that just for you or family too?"

If Private/Limited: "Those usually cap benefits. Were you aware of that?"

Qualify

What’s your zip code and date of birth?

Anyone else you’re looking to cover? If yes, get ages.

Any medications prescribed in the last 12 months?

Any hospital visits or surgeries in the past 7–10 years?

Roughly what’s your household income for 2025, before taxes?

Are you looking to start solo coverage or a family plan?

Passing Run

Perfect, based on the information you’ve given me, I can definitely help put you in a great position for coverage.

I’m going to get you over to my product specialist to go through all of your options.

They’ll be calling from an 832 number—go ahead and answer when it comes in. I’ll stay right here with you until they connect.
